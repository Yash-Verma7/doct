Our Story

A Legacy of Healing, Built on Trust

Founded in 2010, Anti-Gravity Health Clinic began with a singular vision — to create a healthcare institution where medical excellence meets genuine compassion. Our founder, Dr. Vikram Patel, envisioned a clinic that would "elevate" healthcare beyond the ordinary, making world-class medical treatment accessible to every patient.

Over 15 years, we have grown from a modest clinic to a comprehensive healthcare center housing over 40 specialist doctors across six major departments. Every step of our journey has been guided by our commitment to putting patients first.

Today, Anti-Gravity Health Clinic stands as a beacon of trust in the community, serving thousands of patients annually with a 98% satisfaction rate — a testament to our unwavering dedication to quality care.
