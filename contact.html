FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?+
You can book an appointment through our online booking form, call us at +91 123-456-7890, or message us on WhatsApp. Our team will confirm your appointment within 30 minutes during working hours.
What insurance plans do you accept?+
We accept all major insurance providers including Star Health, HDFC ERGO, ICICI Lombard, Max Bupa, Bajaj Allianz, New India, Aditya Birla, and Tata AIG. Please carry your insurance card during your visit.
Is emergency service available 24/7?+
Yes, our emergency department is operational 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays. For medical emergencies, call our emergency line at +91 123-456-7891 or visit our clinic directly.
What should I bring to my first visit?+
Please bring: a valid photo ID, any previous medical records or reports, a list of current medications, your insurance card (if applicable), and arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment for registration.
Do you offer telemedicine consultations?+
Yes, we offer video consultations for follow-up appointments and non-emergency consultations. You can request a telemedicine appointment through our booking form or by calling our reception.
